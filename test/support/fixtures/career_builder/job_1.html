Job Description

Senior Ruby/Rails Developer
Experis IT is seeking a Ruby/Rails Developer with experience in Angular (including ES6+/ Nodejs / angular 2+) and Rails (Ruby/Rails 5+) for a 12 month contract 90% REMOTE opportunity. Candidates need to be able to come onsite to the Dallas/Fort Worth Texas office once a month.
Responsibilities:
Design and implement in house web and/or mobile applications and contributes to architectural discussions and decisions.
Lead the design, development and troubleshooting functionality of web and/or mobile application components.
Leads the development and maintenance of automated unit test cases
Assists leading the discussion with the team and other Engineering staff to accomplish all daily engineering tasks on-time with a high level of quality
Ensures either all defects are addressed in the project lifecycle resulting in the resolution of the defect or the defect being moved to a future release
Assesses defects and determines how they could be prevented.
Continue to advance knowledge and skills through training and mentoring and participates in the training of mentoring of team members
Participate in daily stand ups and other team meetings
Initiates and participates in the development of end-user documentation
Assists with product demonstrations prior to release
Maintains advanced knowledge of technologies related to web and mobile applications and shares that knowledge with their team.
Complies with policies, procedures, and standards defined by the IT department and company and ensures their team does as well.
Has advanced knowledge of complex concepts, practices, and procedures within the testing field and shares that knowledge with their team
Advance knowledge and skills of other engineers through training and mentoring
Assists and provides guidance on the duties performed by other engineers
Skills
Four-year degree in Computer science or Business, or equivalent experience
Five - ten years related experience preferred in software engineering or the development of applications
Proven knowledge of multiple object oriented development languages
Proven knowledge of Agile
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Ability to effectively interact with end- users, peers, managers, and vendors
Self -starter with high level of initiative
Ability to pay high attention to detail, troubleshoot and analyze
Strong commitment to department and company goals
Relies on experience and judgement to plan and accomplish goals and helps team to plan and accomplish theirs.

Experis is an Equal Opportunity Employer (EOE/AA)
